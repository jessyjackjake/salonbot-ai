SalonBot AI

WhatsApp Chatbot for Salon Appointment Booking

SalonBot AI is a smart WhatsApp-based chatbot designed for hair salons and grooming businesses. It helps customers book appointments, view service prices, check opening hours, and communicate with salon staff through an automated conversational system.

This project was developed as a university computing project to demonstrate the use of WhatsApp Cloud API, webhooks, chatbot automation, and intelligent customer interaction for small businesses in the grooming industry.

Features include automated replies, appointment handling, customer service support, and integration with cloud-based messaging infrastructure.
